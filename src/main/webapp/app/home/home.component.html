About SARVM

Sarvm scholarship scheme aims to give scholarship to those students who are well capable to achieve their goals but they don't have enough money to fund their education. sarvm scholarship scheme tries to be your helping hand so that students who are capable, don't have to face difficulties in achieving goals. Government organisations are giving many scholarship on the basis of caste and creed but Sarvm scholarship scheme gives fair chance to every student who are in need of money for achieving their goals. Sarvm scholarship scheme is conducting a examination in whole bihar and on the basis of this examination we are going to give scholarship to meritious student.
